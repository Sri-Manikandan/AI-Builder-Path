# Requesting a New Laptop

## Eligibility
- New hires receive a laptop automatically as part of onboarding.
- Existing employees are eligible for a refresh every 3 years, or sooner if the
  current device is faulty.

## How to request

1. Open the **IT Service Portal** and select **Hardware Request > Laptop**.
2. Choose a standard configuration:
   - **Standard**: 14" laptop, 16 GB RAM, 512 GB SSD (most roles).
   - **Engineering**: 16" laptop, 32 GB RAM, 1 TB SSD (developers, designers).
3. Provide a business justification and your manager's name for approval.
4. Your manager approves the request in the portal.

## Timeline
- Approved standard laptops ship within 5 business days.
- Engineering configurations may take up to 10 business days.
- You will receive a tracking email once the device is dispatched.

For urgent replacements (lost/stolen/broken), contact the IT Service Desk at
ext. 4357 to request a loaner.
