# Payroll Schedule

## When payroll is processed

Presidio runs payroll on a **semi-monthly** schedule:

- **15th of the month** — covers the 1st through the 15th.
- **Last business day of the month** — covers the 16th through month-end.

If a pay date falls on a weekend or public holiday, payroll is deposited on the
**preceding business day**.

## Pay slips
- Available in **Workday** under `Pay > Payslips` on the pay date.
- Direct deposits typically clear by 9:00 AM local time.

## Changes
- Update bank details or tax withholding in Workday at least **5 business days**
  before the next pay date for changes to take effect that cycle.
- For payroll discrepancies, contact `payroll@presidio.internal` within 7 days
  of the pay date.
