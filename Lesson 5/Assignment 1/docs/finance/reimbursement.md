# Filing a Reimbursement

Presidio uses **Concur** for expense reimbursements.

## How to file

1. Log in to Concur via SSO at `https://concur.presidio.internal`.
2. Click **New Expense Report** and give it a descriptive name
   (e.g. "Client visit — May 2026").
3. Add each expense line item and attach a receipt (photo or PDF).
4. Select the correct expense category and project/cost center code.
5. Submit the report to your manager for approval.

## Policy
- Receipts are **required** for any expense over $25.
- Submit reports within **30 days** of the expense date.
- Approved reimbursements are paid in the next payroll cycle via direct deposit.
- Travel must follow the corporate travel policy (economy airfare, standard
  hotel rates).
