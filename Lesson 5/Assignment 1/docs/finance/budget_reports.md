# Budget Reports

## Where to find them

Monthly budget reports are published in the **Finance SharePoint** under
`Finance > Reports > Monthly Budget`.

- Reports are posted by the **5th business day** of each month for the prior
  month.
- Last month's report is always the most recent file in the folder, named
  `Budget_YYYY_MM.xlsx`.

## Access

- Department heads and their delegates have access by default.
- To request access, submit a **Finance Access Request** in the service portal;
  approval comes from the Finance Operations team.

## What's included
- Actual vs. budgeted spend by department and cost center.
- Variance analysis and commentary.
- Forecast for the remaining quarter.

For a custom report or historical data beyond 12 months, email
`finance@presidio.internal`.
